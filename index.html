Approach

How I think about content

I don’t really think of content as just pages or posts. It’s more like a system.

If the structure is off, it doesn’t matter how good the content is — people won’t find it, and teams won’t be able to build on it.

Most of the work I’ve done has been cleaning that up: figuring out how things should be organized, where content is missing, and how to make it easier to scale without starting over every time.
